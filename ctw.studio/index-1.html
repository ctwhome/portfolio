Scrollytelling dossier

How we escort research ideas into public life.

Every engagement is approached as a long-form study. We stay embedded with PIs, archivists, clinicians, and civic technologists. The notes here capture the arc most collaborations follow.

Orientation

Cartography & critical reading

We map datasets, institutional constraints, and scholarly intent. Workshops blend ethnographic interviews with architecture reviews so hypotheses and infrastructure decisions are co-authored with the research collective.

Field systems

Prototyping as publication

Rapid instruments—computational notebooks, VR scenography, participatory data portals—are released to the cohort. We seek critique from advisory boards and domain historians before code freezes.

Operations

Production & care

Systems harden through reproducible pipelines, peer-reviewed security, and rituals for governance. Institutional partners receive stewardship playbooks—documentation written for both engineers and archivists.

Continuum

Culture-making

Launch is framed as scholarship. Exhibitions, symposium demos, and open-source releases turn software into a public artifact with citation-ready provenance.
